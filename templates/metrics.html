Evaluation Methodology

Precision@K

Measures the proportion of recommended items that are relevant. Higher is better.

Precision = (Relevant ∩ Recommended) / K

Recall@K

Measures the proportion of relevant items that were recommended. Higher is better.

Recall = (Relevant ∩ Recommended) / Total Relevant

NDCG@K

Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain - evaluates ranking quality with position discount.

NDCG = DCG / IDCG

Diversity

Average pairwise dissimilarity between recommended items. Higher means more variety.

Diversity = Avg(1 - Similarity(i, j))
